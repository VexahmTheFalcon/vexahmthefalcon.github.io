About This Project

This website publishes structured, independent comparisons of digital compliance and consent tools commonly used by online publishers.

The goal is to summarize publicly available documentation in a clear, neutral, and accessible format for bloggers and website owners evaluating implementation options.

No vendor relationships influence editorial content unless explicitly disclosed.

Information is reviewed periodically based on vendor documentation updates. However, website owners remain responsible for independently verifying compliance requirements.

Editorial Approach

  • Information sourced from official vendor documentation
  • No claims of guaranteed compliance
  • Neutral tone and structured comparison format
  • Regular review of major feature updates
