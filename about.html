About This Project

This website publishes structured, independent comparisons of digital compliance and consent management tools commonly used by online publishers.

The goal is to summarize publicly available vendor documentation in a clear, neutral, and accessible format for bloggers and website owners evaluating implementation options.

This project is independently created and maintained by Deepan N Balaji.

What This Site Covers

Content focuses on consent management platforms, regulatory frameworks, and implementation considerations relevant to websites serving users in jurisdictions such as the European Union.

Each page is designed to function as a structured reference rather than a recommendation or endorsement.

Data Sources

All information is based on publicly available vendor documentation, official product pages, and related technical resources at the time of writing.

Features, pricing, and capabilities may change over time. Readers should verify details directly from official sources before making decisions.

Editorial Approach

  • Information sourced from official vendor documentation
  • No claims of guaranteed compliance
  • Neutral tone and structured comparison format
  • No vendor affiliations unless explicitly stated
  • Periodic review of major feature updates

Limitations and Responsibility

This website does not provide legal advice and does not certify compliance with any regulation, including GDPR or ePrivacy requirements.

Website owners remain responsible for independently evaluating legal obligations and ensuring correct implementation of consent mechanisms.

Update Policy

Content is reviewed periodically based on publicly available documentation. Updates are made when significant changes to features, frameworks, or regulations are identified.
